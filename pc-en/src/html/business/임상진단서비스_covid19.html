Axen™ COVID-19 RT Test Kit

An in vitro diagnostic kit for confirming the infection of COVID-19 in just two hours.

Based on the RT-PCR (Real Time PCR), the kit is suitable for screening patients with small amounts of novel coronavirus due to its outstanding target detection. The Axen™ COVID-19
test kit has obtained export approval from the Korean Ministry of Food and Drug Safety and Europe’s In-Vitro Diagnostic (CE-IVD) to provide reliable diagnostic services.

Macrogen Smart Mobile Lab

Smart Mobile Lab is the first-ever on-site COVID 19 testing system developed in Korea.

This is a small mobile lab for on-site testing of the virus, and it is composed of an all-in-one system that can perform the entire process, starting from the extraction of virus nucleic acid,
followed by genetic testing, and then the analysis results.
